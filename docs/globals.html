APE.color.ColorRGB.blend

<static>  APE.color.ColorRGB.blend(startColor, endColor, rationalValue, mixedColor)
    Takes a midpoint color between two colors. Useful for animation.
    Parameters:
      ColorRGB startColor - the color to transition from.
      ColorRGB endColor - the color to transition to.
      ufloat rationalValue - ratio [0..1] of endColor:startColor color for result.
      ColorRGB mixedColor? - - a color can be passed in for reuse (this is useful for animation, to avoid creation of many objects).
    Returns:
      APE.color.ColorRGB new color that is a blend of rationalValue/1 new color and 1-rationalValue startColor.
